Persona Descriptions

Persona 1: Darnell

Quote

"A friend sent me mental health resources, but I'm not really comfortable going to therapy yet. I think I'd like to just see what's out there for now."

Core Needs

  • To be able to access mental health care information privately and securely.
  • Access to accurate and culturally sensitive information about trauma and depression.
  • To see providers and services offerred by people who understand his experiences.

Persona 2: Emily

Quote

“I honestly think I would prefer if someone just recommended providers to me. I even created a Finsta to ask for suggestions.”

Core Needs

  • To find a therapist that speaks Mandarin.
  • Guidance about how to talk to her family about mental health.
  • To feel supported by a social network.
  • To understand payment options and find a provider that accepts her parents insurance.

Persona 3

Quote

“I don’t want to navigate the additional barrier of explaining my identities. I need some common ground with my therapist to feel comfortable.”

Core Needs

  • To find a provider that identifies as South Asian, preferably in the LGBTQ+ community.
  • A quick and efficient process that doesn’t cause additional stress.
  • To determine the best type of therapy for her preferences and find a therapist qualified to provide treatment.
